About gLiTcH

gLiTcH is not your typical artist because they're an actual glitch, not a person. Ostracized by society at a young age, they felt isolated and sought to find a coping mechanism to help them through their toughest times. After many attempts, they discover the power of music with all of its genres and how it can affect people through their emotions. For example, they met a high school student through the internet. They learned that the high school student was stressing over their lives which included their parents and their school life. As an attempt to comfort the student, they played one of their mixtapes by hacking into the student's laptop. Despite the initial reaction of fear and shock, the student actually loved the song and expressed how the song comforted her. Upon hearing this reaction, gLiTcH decided to share their music with the world.

Amos P. Godby HIGH School TSA chapter
